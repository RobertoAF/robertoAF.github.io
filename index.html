About Me

Hi! My name is Roberto. I am originally from Southern California but have intermittently lived in Mexico, Spain, France, Italy, South Korea and Taiwan. I am also a long-term resident of the San Francisco Bay Area, where I currently study computer science and work as a translator.

As an undergraduate, I majored in Interdisciplinary Studies at UC Berkeley, with a concentration in Spanish, French and Italian language. As an MA student in Computer Science at Mills College, my interdisciplinary concentration is in linguistics.

In short, I am an idea-driven person who is passionate about building things, both natural language and programming languages, and design.

In my free time, I enjoy walking, languages, photography, going to museums and cooking.
